Steve Jobs

Co-founder of Apple Inc.

Steve Jobs was an American business magnate, industrial designer, and inventor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and chief executive officer (CEO) of Apple Inc., and is widely recognized as a pioneer of the personal computer revolution and the modern smartphone.
